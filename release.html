overview

Collaborating with an organisations to bring new experiences for university students through digital means. 

objective

Create a step by step process for students to feel comfortable releasing themselves from worrying things that may be happening with their lives.

While doing so, we offer them art therapy exer-cises, creative practices and techniques that they can then take into their daily life in order to un-derstand themselves better and not reach mental crisis.

wireframe

The intial idea was to have the website be a single page with all the main sections that we really needed to show.
